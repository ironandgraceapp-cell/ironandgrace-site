Our Mission

Where Discipline Meets Devotion

Iron & Grace was born from a simple conviction: physical training and spiritual growth are not separate pursuits. When you push through a hard set, you build more than muscle — you build character, perseverance, and the kind of discipline that honors God.

We believe your time matters. Every workout should be efficient, intentional, and purposeful. Iron & Grace optimizes your training so you make the best use of every minute in the gym — and transforms the moments between sets into opportunities to grow in faith. Rest periods become scripture moments. Downtime becomes devotion.

We created this app for believers who want their time in the gym to be as intentional as their time in the Word. No wasted reps. No wasted rest. One mission.

"Train yourself to be godly. For physical training is of some value, but godliness has value for all things." — 1 Timothy 4:7-8

Features

Everything You Need to Train with Purpose

Powerful fitness tools built on a foundation of faith. Every feature is designed to strengthen both your body and your walk with Christ.

🤖

AI-Powered Coaching

A personal coach that knows your plan, your progress, and your goals. Get real-time guidance rooted in proven training principles and Biblical encouragement.

📝

Smart Workout Plans

AI-generated periodized programs tailored to your experience level, schedule, and goals. Or build your own custom workouts from a library of 180+ exercises.

🛡

Armor of God Achievements

Unlock Biblical achievement cards as you train — from the Belt of Truth to the Sword of the Spirit. Each card ties your physical milestones to spiritual armor.

📕

Scripture-Integrated Training

Start each workout with prayer. Encounter scripture woven into your training experience. Let every rep be an act of worship.

⏱

Faith-Fueled Rest Timer

Smart rest timers keep your workouts efficient and on pace. Between sets, engage with scripture, devotionals, and faith-building content — turning every rest period into spiritual growth.

📈

Progress Tracking

Track PRs, volume, streaks, bodyweight, and macros. See your growth over time with detailed history and stats that celebrate consistency.

🏆

Discipleship Cards

Earn character cards inspired by Biblical figures — from David's courage to Esther's faithfulness. Your fitness journey mirrors the heroes of faith.
